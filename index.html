About us

JAR4U is a leading jewellery technology company that provides an end-to-end solution for jewellery retailers and manufacturers. We use advanced AI and 3D modeling techniques to create photorealistic and non-editable 3D models of jewellery. These models are then displayed in augmented reality (AR) to give customers an accurate and immersive shopping experience. By taking input videos from jewelers, we are able to create 3D models that are an exact replica of the original jewellery piece. With JAR4U, customers can try on jewellery in the comfort of their own home and see how it looks and feels on them before making a purchase. This allows them to make an informed decision and ensures that they are getting the best quality and value for their money. Upgrade your jewellery game with JAR4U today!
